THE AT03


Sporting a 1.6 L (98 cu in) direct injection V6 turbocharged engine limited to 15,000 rpm in a mid-mounted, rear-wheel drive layout the AT03 is an exciting machine out on the track. It also has Brembo 6-piston aluminium-lithium calipers, Brembo carbon discs and carbon pads, and Red Bull Powertrains kinetic and thermal energy recovery systems. Performance is paramount.
